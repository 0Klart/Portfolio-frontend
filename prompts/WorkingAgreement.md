# Working Agreement

## Delivery principle

- Stabilize functionality before polishing content.
- Fix broken integrations before making them look better.
- Prefer public proof points over generic portfolio claims.

## Collaboration pattern

- Research public profile data first.
- Apply content once facts are confirmed.
- Keep backend and deployment assumptions synchronized with frontend changes.

## Review standard

- No placeholder text in completed sections.
- No broken links or broken fetch flows.
- No CV-only or private information added without an explicit decision.
